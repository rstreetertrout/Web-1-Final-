About me-

I am a aspiring designer, innovator and vissionary. I was raised in a small town in Minnesota and as an only child I had plenty of free time to draw. By the time my parents bought me my first iPad it wasn’t video games that interested me, it was my best friend Jack and I comparing notes as we watched shoe reviews, shoe design videos and chatted about the latest Nikes we were going to buy.

I spend my free time free hand drawing hopefully the next great shoe. My designs are based on my own experience as a runner and avid shoe collector, keeping in mind that I’m just as interested in comfort as I am in style.

My goal is to start my career as a footwear creative lending my talent to a team looking for fresh ideas, a committed team player lending my attention to detail and creative input.
